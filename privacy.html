Privacy Policy

What we collect

Account: your email address and the username you choose (handled by our authentication provider, Supabase).

Location: when you open the map or check in, the app uses your device location to show nearby temples and to verify that a check-in happened on site. Location is used only while you are using the app (when-in-use).

Camera & photos: when you create a check-in, the photo you capture or select is uploaded to your account.

Content you create: check-ins, ratings and reviews you post. Saved (wishlisted) temples are stored only on your device.

How we use it

To run core features: showing nearby temples, verifying and displaying your check-ins, collecting stamps, and showing reviews. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use third-party advertising trackers.

Storage & deletion

Your data is stored with Supabase. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Delete account.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data.

Contact

Questions about privacy: hello@theway.app
